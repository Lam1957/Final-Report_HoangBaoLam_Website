About Kson

Our Story

Welcome to Kson, where elegance meets exquisite craftsmanship. Established in 2017, we we have 8 years of experience in crafting powerful jewelry that exudes beauty and durability over time. Our journey began with a passion for designing unique pieces that tell a story, celebrate love, and capture life's most precious moments.

At Kson, we believe that every piece of jewelry should reflect the uniqueness of the wearer. Therefore, we work closely with talented people to create collections that combine modern trends with classic designs, ensuring that each piece is a unique work of art.

Our Team

Our team is a family of passionate designers, craftsmen and jewellers, dedicated to bringing you the best. Led by our creators, Karen and Yvonne, we combine creativity and expertise to create the perfect piece of jewellery for our customers. From sourcing the finest quality materials to the finishing touches, our floral team is committed to delivering exquisite craftsmanship in every piece.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the finest materials to ensure every piece is of the highest standard.
  • Creativity: Our designs are inspired by the beauty of nature, love, and modern elegance.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Your happiness is our priority, and we strive to provide exceptional service.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to ethical sourcing and sustainable practices in all our operations.
